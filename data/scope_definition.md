- Internal Combustion Engine with Automatic transmission in a Test cell at PV

Inputs:
- Engine start/stop
- Gas Pedal position
- Gear stick position
- Drive mode Convenient & Sport

Outputs:
- Gear Indicator
- Gearbox RPM
- Engine RPM
- Fault message (e.g. if CAN messages stops)

Out of scope:
- Vehicle speed
- Air resistent
- Antispinn
- Fuel pump
